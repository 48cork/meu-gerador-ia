# AIOS Design System: Arbitrage Pro

This document establishes the UI/UX standards for the **Arbitrage Pro** application. All future modifications must adhere to these guidelines to ensure visual consistency and professional identity.

## 1. Header & Branding
- **Logo**: Must be properly centered at the top of the main container.
- **Title**: "Agente Arbitragem Pro" must be centered below or alongside the logo.
- **Color**: Primary Green (#4CAF50).

## 2. Sidebar (Suporte)
- **Section**: Must include a "Suporte e Dúvidas" header.
- **Action**: Must contain a direct link button to WhatsApp.
- **Link Format**: `https://wa.me/5583991183123?text=...` (Preserve the exact tracking text).

## 3. Navigation (Tabs)
The application is strictly divided into three markets:
1. **🇧🇷 Brasil**: Local Marketplaces (BRL).
2. **🇺🇸 ClickBank**: International Affiliate Market (USD -> BRL).
3. **🇪🇺 Digistore24**: European Affiliate Market (EUR -> BRL).
*Do not mix contexts without explicit separation.*

## 4. Footer
- **Position**: Fixed at the bottom of the viewport.
- **Content**: "© 2026 Arbitrage Pro - Desenvolvido por Sérgio".
- **Style**: Grey text on light background, centered.
